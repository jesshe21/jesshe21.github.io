My story

I grew up in Rochester, MN—nestled between Mayo Clinic and IBM. Surrounded by research and engineering, I carved out my own path by leaning into creative problem solving and community building.

After moving 1,800 miles to attend UC Berkeley, I fell in love with teaching, cultural exchange, and the thrill of building something meaningful from scratch. Since then I've been based in San Francisco, working in finance and partnering with people who are shaping what's next.

If you’d like to hop on a call or meet for coffee, I’d love to connect.

About Me

I grew up in cozy Rochester, MN, sandwiched between the two large employers in town: Mayo Clinic (where my mom worked) and IBM (where my dad worked). Eager to escape the cold, I moved 1800 miles west to attend Berkeley.

Of my time in college, I fondly remember teaching an origami course, interning in Ireland, and bombarding Sproul Plaza passersby with free Exo cricket bars as a campus ambassador.

Today I work in VC in San Francisco. If you'd like to chat or meet for coffee, I would love to hear from you at jessh1821@gmail.com!

About

Finance is my toolkit, but people, storytelling, and exploration are my fuel.

I grew up in Rochester, Minnesota between the Mayo Clinic and IBM. Surrounded by science and engineering, I learned early how to translate complex ideas into approachable stories—and to stay curious about what might be possible beyond home.

That curiosity took me to UC Berkeley where I studied Operations Research and Business Administration, taught an origami course, interned abroad in Ireland, and championed new experiences on campus as a student leader and ambassador.

Today, I'm in San Francisco helping companies navigate growth through strategic finance. If you’d like to collaborate, grab coffee, or swap stories about big ideas, I’d love to connect.

About

I grew up in Rochester, MN, where Mayo Clinic and IBM shaped the local landscape. After high school, I ventured 1,800 miles west to attend UC Berkeley.

At Berkeley, I taught origami, interned in Ireland, and explored diverse career paths through various student organizations. These experiences helped me discover my passion for finance and strategic problem-solving.

Since graduating, I've been working in finance in San Francisco, most recently at Bolt Financial in strategic finance. Previously, I worked in investment banking at Goldman Sachs focusing on technology, media, and telecommunications.

I'd love to connect. Reach me at jesshe21@berkeley.edu.
